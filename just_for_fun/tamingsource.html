GAME RULES
GAME RULES
Collect resources to craft buildings.
Level up to unlock various items.
Place down windmills to generate gold.
Level up your pets.
Destroy chests around the map to collect a special currency, Golden Apples. You can use them to customize your avatar.
HOW TO TAME PETS IN GAME
HOW TO TAME PETS IN GAME
When you are in-game, find an animal that is sleeping and press the Taming button (a paw). If you succeed, the animal will become your companion.
You can own 3 animals at the same time
Fight other animals and players to level up your pet
Currently, there are 3 pet levels. (Baby, Adult, and Boss) The higher the level, the more health, the faster and damage it deals.
HOW PET ELEMENTS WORK
HOW PET ELEMENTS WORK
Each animal has a specific element.
These elements make it stronger or weaker against certain animals.
To better understand how elements work, select a pet before starting a game and go to "Type and Skill."
HOW TO EARN GOLDEN APPLES
HOW TO EARN GOLDEN APPLES
Find chests around the map and destroy them to earn Golden Apples.
You can also directly buy Golden Apples in the shop.
The final way is to join our Discord Community of Taming.io. There are many events hosted by the developer for you to win extra Golden Apples!
